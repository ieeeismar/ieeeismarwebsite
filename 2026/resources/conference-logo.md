### Usage Notes

These assets are intended for official IEEE ISMAR 2026 communication and promotional materials. When using them, please ensure that the logo remains clearly visible, is not distorted, and is placed on a suitable background.

For best results, use the logo with wordmark when the conference name should be clearly displayed, and use the standalone logo or graphic elements for decorative or supporting visual purposes.
