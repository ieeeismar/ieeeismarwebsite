The official IEEE ISMAR 2026 social media frames are available for download below.

The templates are designed around the most common formats used across major social media platforms worldwide: vertical stories, portrait feed posts, and square posts. Exact dimensions may vary slightly between platforms, but the graphic elements have been placed away from the borders so that each frame can be adapted safely when uploading or resizing content.

These materials are intended to support conference-related communication, announcements, and promotional posts. The expected use is simple: download the preferred frame, then place it over your image or video as a sticker or overlay using the editing tools provided by the social media platform.

Click on any frame to download it.

### Usage Notes

Use the vertical templates for stories and reels, the feed templates for portrait-format posts, and the square templates for standard square posts.

When creating content, place your image or video inside the central area of the frame. Keep the conference branding visible and avoid cropping the graphic elements whenever possible.

The frames are designed with safe margins so they can be adapted to slightly different platform requirements without losing the main visual identity.
