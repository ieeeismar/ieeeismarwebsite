Puglia forms the heel of the Italian peninsula and has long been a vibrant crossroads between East and West. Around Bari, visitors can explore dramatic cliffs, sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, limestone caves, ancient towns,
medieval castles, olive landscapes and UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The region is ideal for short independent excursions, relaxed seaside visits and full-day trips before or after the event.
