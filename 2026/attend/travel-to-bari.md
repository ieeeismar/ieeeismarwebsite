## Air Connections

<div class="travel-section">
  <figure class="travel-image">
    <img src="{{ travel_img_path | append: 'aeroporto-bari.jpg' | relative_url }}" alt="Bari Karol Wojtyła Airport" loading="lazy">
  </figure>

  <p>Bari can be easily reached through <a href="https://bari.airports.aeroportidipuglia.it/en/" target="_blank">Bari Karol Wojtyła Airport (BRI)</a>, which offers national and international connections. Participants arriving from Europe may consider direct flights to Bari or connecting flights through other Italian or European airports.</p>

  <p>Nearby airports may also be considered:</p>

  <ul>
    <li>Brindisi Airport (BDS): connected to Bari by train or coach. From there, you can take a direct shuttle bus to Bari Centrale, the main railway station of Bari. The service is operated by <a href="https://shop.marozzivt.it/en/s/Brindisi%20Aeroporto/260609/to/Bari%20Aeroporto?adulti=1" target="_blank">Marozzi</a>, and tickets can be purchased online for €10.00. The journey takes approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes. Alternatively, you can reach Brindisi Centrale railway station and then take a train to Bari Centrale.</li>

    <li>Naples Airport (NAP): connected to Bari by train or coach from Naples city centre.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

### If you come from outside Europe

<p>Participants arriving from outside Europe may consider connecting flights through major Italian airports, such as:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Flight to Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO): From Rome Fiumicino, you can take a direct flight to Bari Karol Wojtyła Airport (BRI). There are usually several direct flights per day, operated by <a href="https://www.ita-airways.com/gb/en/homepage" target="_blank">ITA Airways</a> and <a href="https://www.ryanair.com/us/en" target="_blank">Ryanair</a>. The average flight time is approximately 1 hour.</li>

  <li>Flight to Milan Malpensa Airport (MXP): From Milan Malpensa, you can take a direct flight to Bari Karol Wojtyła Airport (BRI). There are usually several direct flights per day, operated by <a href="https://www.ryanair.com/us/en" target="_blank">Ryanair</a> and <a href="https://www.easyjet.com/en" target="_blank">easyJet</a>. The average flight time is approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes.</li>
</ul>

## Train Connections

<div class="travel-section">
  <figure class="travel-image">
    <img src="{{ travel_img_path | append: 'train.webp' | relative_url }}" alt="Train connections to Bari Centrale Railway Station" loading="lazy">
  </figure>

  <p>Bari Centrale Railway Station is connected to major Italian cities by high-speed and long-distance trains, including Rome, Naples, and Milan. The journey from Rome takes approximately 4 hours. Participants can check timetables and purchase tickets through <a href="https://www.trenitalia.com/en.html" target="_blank">Trenitalia</a> and <a href="https://www.italotreno.com/en" target="_blank">Italo</a>.</p>
</div>

## Coach Services

<p>Bari is also connected to many Italian cities by long-distance coach services. Coach operators include <a href="https://www.flixbus.ie/?noRedirect=true" target="_blank">FlixBus</a>, <a href="https://www.itabus.it/en/home" target="_blank">Itabus</a>, <a href="https://shop.marozzivt.it/en/s/Brindisi%20Aeroporto/260609/to/Bari%20Aeroporto?adulti=1" target="_blank">Marozzi</a>, and <a href="https://marinobus.it/en/" target="_blank">MarinoBus</a>. Coaches stop at Terminal Bus Station near Bari Centrale Railway Station, in the Via Giuseppe Capruzzi area.</p>
