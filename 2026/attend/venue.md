## Conference Venue: Nicolaus Hotel

The conference will be organized at The Nicolaus Hotel, an elegant conference center and hotel a few step away from Bari city center, Puglia, Italy.

The Nicolaus Hotel offers a welcoming atmosphere and an immediate sense of belonging. The interiors are full of light, with functional and technological spaces combined with art and creativity. The venue has a large Congress Centre divided into 15 modular and technical conference rooms seating up to 1000 and qualified professional staff to support the conference’s activities.

## Ways to Reach Venue

The <strong>conference venue</strong> can be reached from the Bari city centre in different ways:
* Bus: There are several lines which stop near the venue, we suggest using line 6; you can get precise directions using <a href="https://moovitapp.com/">Moovit</a>

* Taxi: A ride takes 5-10 minutes and costs 10-15€

* Renting mobility services: Bari offers various electric bike/scooter/car renting services where you can pick up a vehicle at one location and drop it off at another in city districts; some examples are <a href="https://www.vaimoo.app/it/bari-bike-sharing/">Vaimoo</a>, <a href="https://bitmobility.it/en/app-bit-mobility-electric-scooter-bari/">Bit Mobility</a>, and <a href="https://www.pikyrent.com/en/sharing/">Pikyrent</a>

* Walking: You can enjoy a nice walk of about 3-4 km (40-60 minutes): you will walk along two tree-lined avenues, "<a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/36Bw5hog1WLfTP6J6">Viale della Repubblica</a>" and "<a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/KfWFSny24HupiZpHA">Viale Giovanni XXIII</a>" that intersect in the main Bari's park, "<a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/8DZ3v4zMocDv13Bt6">Parco 2 giugno</a>;" we suggest to arrive in Poggiofranco through the recently opened "<a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/qRHMcfrg99NfLHZy9">Via Mazzitelli</a>", with plenty of nice restaurants and new buildings
