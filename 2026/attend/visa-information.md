## Visa information 

Participants are responsible for checking whether they need an entry visa to travel to Italy. The official Visa for Italy portal of the Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation provides a guided procedure to verify visa requirements based on nationality, country of residence, reason for visit, and length of stay. If a visa is required, the portal provides the relevant information and forms needed to submit the application. 

Conference participants are strongly encouraged to check visa requirements well in advance of the conference. To avoid delays, participants who need a visa should start the application process as early as possible. 

For short stays, the Schengen visa covers stays of up to 90 days within any 180 days. Participants who need a visa should contact the Italian Embassy or Consulate responsible for their place of residence well in advance of their trip. 

Verify via official portal of <a href="https://vistoperitalia.esteri.it/?lang=en_US" target="_blank">Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation</a>.

## Visa Invitation Letter 

* Important: If you request a visa invitation letter, your registration cannot be cancelled.

During registration, you will have the option to request a visa invitation letter. If selected, it will be automatically generated upon completion of your registration, using the name, organization, address, passport number, and planned arrival and departure dates in Italy that you provide.

If you require additional information on the letter (e.g., date of birth or passport details such as number, date of issue, expiration date, and place of issue), please email the ISMAR 2026 Registration Chairs <a href="registration2026@ieeeismar.net">registration2026@ieeeismar.net</a>.

* For Journal and Conference Paper Authors
We strongly encourage authors to register immediately after receiving the final notification. Visa invitation letters will be issued as soon as possible. In any case, we advise authors to anticipate potential delays in the visa application process.
