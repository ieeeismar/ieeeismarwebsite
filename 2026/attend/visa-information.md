## Visa information 

Participants are responsible for checking whether they need an entry visa to travel to Italy. The official visa for Italy portal of the <a href="https://vistoperitalia.esteri.it/?lang=en_US" target="_blank">Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation</a> provides a guided procedure to verify visa requirements based on nationality, country of residence, reason for visit, and length of stay. If a visa is required, the portal provides the relevant information and forms needed to submit the application. Once the procedure is completed, if a visa invitation letter is necessary, please send a formal request to the ISMAR 2026 Registration Chairs at [registration2026@ieeeismar.net](mailto:registration2026@ieeeismar.net).

The Registration Chairs will reply by providing a template to be completed with all the information required to issue the visa support letter. 

Conference participants are strongly encouraged to check visa requirements well in advance of the conference. To avoid delays, participants who need a visa should start the application process as early as possible.

For short stays, the Schengen VISA covers stays of up to 90 days within any 180 days.

> **Important:** If you request a visa invitation letter, your registration cannot be cancelled.
